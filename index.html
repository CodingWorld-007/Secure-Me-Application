HOW TO
USE IT

Your data stays only on your device. Here’s how SecureMe protects your data from the moment you open the app.

01
First Launch — Security Setup
🔐
When you open SecureMe for the first time, the app sets up your personal security system. Your fingerprint or phone lock becomes the key to your vault. Only you can unlock it. No one else, not even the app developers.
02
Daily Use — Unlock Vault
🗄️
Just use your fingerprint to unlock the vault. Once you close the app or leave it for some time, it automatically locks again so no one can access your data without you.
03
Saving — Your Data
✍️
Everything you save — passwords, notes, documents — is stored in a locked format that cannot be read by any other app or person. Only your SecureMe vault can open it.
04
Double Lock — Extra Security
🛡️
You can add an extra password to any important entry. So even if someone unlocks your vault, they still cannot open that entry without the second password.
05
Backup — Your Control
📦
You can create a backup file and store it anywhere you want — Google Drive, WhatsApp, Email, Telegram, or a pen drive. The backup file is fully protected and cannot be opened without your permission.
06
Import — Restore Anywhere
📲
If you change your phone, just open the backup file with SecureMe and your vault will be restored in seconds. No account, no server, no data tracking — everything stays in your control.

PRIVACY
POLICY

Last updated: March 2026. This policy applies to the SecureMe Android application published by Virtual Look.

01 //
Data Collection

SecureMe does not collect, store, or send any of your personal data anywhere.

Everything you save — passwords, notes, documents, and other private information — stays only on your device.

We do not collect your data. We do not see your data. Your data stays with you.
02 //
Internet Access

SecureMe works without internet access.

The app cannot send your data anywhere. It works completely offline on your device.

03 //
Local Storage

SecureMe stores your data securely on your device so only you can access it.

  • Your secure vault data
  • Your security settings
  • Your backup settings

No other app, company, or person can access this data.

04 //
Backup Files

SecureMe allows you to create backup files so you can restore your data later.

  • Backup files are protected
  • Backup files are stored where you choose
  • Backup files are shared only if you choose to share them
You control where your backup goes and who can access it.
05 //
Biometric Data

SecureMe can use your fingerprint or phone lock to unlock the app.

Your fingerprint or face data is handled by your phone’s system, not by SecureMe. We never see or store your biometric data.

06 //
Third-Party Libraries

SecureMe uses some trusted tools to make the app work properly, such as the database, user interface, and app updates.

These tools do not collect your personal data from SecureMe.

07 //
Children's Privacy · Changes · Contact

Children's Privacy: SecureMe does not collect data from anyone, including children.
